Choose from thousands of themes Our community provides thousands of colourschemes, icons, window and desktop styles, splash screens - brought right to your desktop with a click of a button.

Available in over 50 languages Translations are constantly being updated every version to introduce new languages.

Covers the basics Familiar and easy to pick up. No nasty suprises.

Type & Go Begin typing, and search, launch apps, calculate, browse the web, and more.
